Services

Curently, our project can scan a nutrition label and read the calories using Tesseract.js. Users can also input their daily health goals. Using that information, it will tell you how many calories you have consumed that day and whether you've reached your goal or not.

Future Goals

  • We want to be able to read more information from the nutrition label and create more statistics from those.
  • Create an account system.
  • Add a function that tells people whether or not a food is healthy.
  • We'd also like to inform our clients on what each part of the label and the ingredients mean.
  • Lastly, we're hoping to add a database about the average calories for most foods because not every food has a nutrition label to scan.
